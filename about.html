Empire of the Sun is a strategic level look at the entire War in the Pacific from the attack on Pearl Harbor until the surrender of Japan. This is the first card driven game (CDG) to move the system closer to a classic hexagon wargame, while retaining all of the tension and uncertainty people have come to expect from a CDG. Players are cast in the role of MacArthur, Yamamoto, Nimitz, and Mountbatten as you direct your forces across the breadth of the globe from India to Hawaii and from Alaska to Australia.

Designer: Mark Herman.
